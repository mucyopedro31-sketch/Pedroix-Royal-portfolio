About Me 🌟

My name is King Pedro, and I am a curious person who enjoys exploring new ideas and learning about the world around me. I am especially interested in science, technology, and art, because each of them allows me to understand, create, and express ideas in different ways.

One of the projects I am especially proud of is my biohabitat project, which focused on sustainability and the relationship between nature and design. Working on this project helped me develop important skills such as planning, problem-solving, and creative thinking. It also deepened my interest in exploring how science and creativity can work together to solve real-world challenges. In addition to this project, I also launched my own personal portfolio website, where I share my ideas, interests, and learning journey in science, technology, and art.

For me, learning is like an adventure — every step brings new discoveries and new opportunities to grow. In my free time, I enjoy coding, drawing, and creating new ideas. I hope that visitors who explore this portfolio can see my curiosity, passion for learning, and excitement for creating new things.
